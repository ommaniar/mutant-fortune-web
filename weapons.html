Weapons

Weapon are used to defeat an enemy. Each weapon consist of unique special ability which can be used to destroy monsters. Weapons can be bought using gems.

Each time weapon is shoot or special ability is used, it consumes energy which can be regilled using a battery.

Feel free to use and explore any weapon which you may find fitting to your playstyle. Equip your favourite weapon and be the number one.

Gold

Gold is powerful sniper and also the costliest. It costs 6000 in armory.

Special Ability: Deals a massive aoe damage.

Pistol

Pistol is the basic weapon given to you from the beginning. It might look weak to you but it is decent enough to clear enemies as per is cost pric.e

Special Ability: Significantly increases Fire Rate.

Poison

Poison is quite it sounds like it throws poison and damages enemy. It cost 2500 ingame currecny and is the cheapest weapon to buy.

Special Ability: Throws a molly which gives damage over time to anyone who is on it.

Blaster

Blaster is a good weapon with better firerate than pistol and it shoots laser out of it. Costs 3000 currency.

Special Ability: Doesn't consume energy for some time also increase damage output.

Bhopu

Bhopu is a trumpet which actually damages via noise. Costs 3500 in armory.

Special Ability: Creates bigger soundwaves for some time.

Canine

Canine shoots a shuriken and deals damage to enemy.It costs 3000 in armory to buy.

Special Ability: Instead of one shuriken it throws multiple shuriken.

Alien

Alien is mysterious gun which shoots a mysterious object and deals damage. Costs 5000 in armory.

Special Ability: Becomes dark and automatically finds enemy and shoot it.
